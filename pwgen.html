What is the expectations?

1. You can get a strong password with easy memorization. You can get the same one in this page.
2. If you stolen a password for one of web sites, your other password would be safe.
3. If one of your passwords has been exposed to someone for a moment, it's not easy to remember so that your password might be safe.

Keep in Mind!

Do not expose your "Easywords" and especially "Password."
If those things are exposed, your strong passwords will become very weak.
